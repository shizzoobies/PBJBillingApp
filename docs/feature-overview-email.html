Just shipped

Newest features
Rock-solid saving. Edits save reliably and the “All changes saved” note only shows when your work is truly saved — no more losing what you were typing.
Bill by team-member rate — including yours. Hourly clients are billed at each person's own rate (set on the Team page), and you can set your own rate so your hours bill too. Team billable hours now report correctly.
Client notes your team can add. A timestamped, signed notes log on each client — you and the client's assigned bookkeeper/accountant can read and add notes.
Bookkeepers can open their clients. Assigned staff get a scoped client view (contacts, checklists, recent work, notes) — billing and financials stay owner-only.
Plans → checklists → board. Link a plan to its standard checklists, then set them up on a client in one click — they land in the right board column automatically.
“To 100%” setup checklist. A live page that shows exactly what's left to finish setting up (missing rates, unlinked contacts, and more), each linking to the fix.
Smarter contacts. One contact across many companies with a per-company email, link related contacts, archive old ones, and flag contacts not tied to any client.
Staff get Delayed & Gantt; safer deletes. Bookkeepers can see Delayed and Gantt (scoped to their clients), and staff deletions of a checklist now need your one-tap approval.
“Due this month” & hours export. Filter a client's active checklists to what's due this month, and download a line-by-line hours-by-month CSV from Reports.

The full toolkit

Everything the app does, by area.
Time & approvals

Live timer or manual entry, per client or split across a group, with admin/internal time. Weekly timesheets, owner approval queue, send-back-with-note, and month-end locking. Manual entries flag for your approval.

Checklists, templates & the board

Tasks per client with steps, sub-steps, due dates and assignees. Recurring templates that auto-generate each period, multi-stage “cases” that route stage to stage, a “waiting on” flag with notify-when-cleared, and the Active Checklists board organized by service type with a week/month/quarter view.

Clients, contacts & plans

Full client profiles (hourly / monthly / annual billing), a shared contacts directory with CSV import, subscription plans that label invoices and drive standard checklists, and per-client notes & recap.

Invoices & reporting

Per-client invoice drafts with reimbursements, branded print or email, and a billing queue. Reports for hours by person/client (with billable $), a productivity heatmap, a Gantt timeline, and CSV exports.

Team, roles & security

Invite bookkeepers, set bill/cost rates, and scope each person to their assigned clients. Sign in by magic link or password with optional two-factor, full activity log, and per-user access control.

AI assistant — chat & voice

Ask questions by typing or by talking out loud. It pulls live numbers (profitability, hours, what's overdue, who's at capacity), builds reports you can save as PDF, remembers facts across calls, and can set a few things up — always behind a one-tap confirmation. It never changes anything on its own.
